About Resume

Background

This spring, I joined a team of student designers in a contracted design project. Interestingly enough, COVID-19 struck mid-way through the semester, resulting in some remote work experience. S/O to Figma for its collaborative features, especially the cursor-following one!

Client Vision

Our team partnered with two Haas MBA students to build a mobile app that streamlined and combined educational mindfulness courses, pain management, and a physical CBD product. This app would help and empower patients with Rheumatoid Arthritis and/or other autoimmune diseases through mindfulness education, meditation exercises, and other tools. Our task was to focus on the UI/UX of the mobile app.
