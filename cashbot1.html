Cashbot.ai Creator Experience

Summer 2018

Visual design for customer-facing experience


I worked within a team to produce hi-fidelity screens for the chatbot creator experience.Due to the fast-paced nature of the start-up, the screens went straight to development, skipping prototyping.

A bit about this app: First, a chatbot needs to be created. Any topic works, so in this case, itâ€™s a chatbot that gives dental hygiene advice. Then, the chatbot creator goes to cashbot.ai and sets up an agent, which is an e-commerce store (e.g. Amazon, Shopify) and its product offerings. Then, the creator can train the agent by optimizing the data set and choosing the relevant products. Perhaps, toothpaste is more appealing to crowds than dentures? When the agent is implemented, products are suggested to the chatbot user and every click-through purchase results in profit for the creator and naturally, the e-commerce store.
